About.

This web (or app) is created to complete our final project of the Sistem Multimedia course in Teknik Informatika UIN Sunan Gunung Djati Bandung. Supposed to detect what facial expressions are made by the user. Created using Flask with CNN algorithm and supported with OpenCV2 tool.
App may be inaccurate and need more improvement. So if you have any suggestion please feel free to tell us!
